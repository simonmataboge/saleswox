Work History/Occupational History

Use recent employment history to old, atleast start with your last job that is relevent to the position you are applying for. Show the recruiter why they should consider you for the position and what makes you stand out from all applicants.Show the recruiter your target market and show them what do you really do and what impact or change will you bring

Salary

Do your research about the position, industry and the company so that you can be realistic about your salary expectation, give reasons as to why you should receive the expected salary

References

Recruiter or HR manager will need to make sure if the information on your cv is truthful and the only way to make sure is by contacting your references. It is advisable to avoid using friends and family, rather keep your cv professional by using professional references
